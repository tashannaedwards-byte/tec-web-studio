About

One studio. One focus.

At TEC Web Studio, we're focused on one thing: helping small businesses, service providers, and organizations get a professional online presence that actually brings in customers.

We got into this work because we kept seeing great professionals and passionate business owners lose customers to competitors who simply had a better-looking website — not better work, just a better online presence.

Our approach is deliberately simple and focused: no bloated agency upsells, no mysterious monthly retainers. You get a professional website built to bring in real customer inquiries, delivered fast, at a fair price.

When you work with us, you're working directly with the people building your site. That means faster communication, fewer misunderstandings, and a site that actually reflects your business.

Sound Familiar?

Most local business websites are quietly costing you customers

If your website was built years ago, or you're relying on Facebook alone, you're likely losing inquiries to competitors every single week — without even knowing it.

  • ✕
    Outdated or amateur-looking design Visitors judge your business in seconds. A poor website signals poor work — even when your actual work is excellent.
  • ✕

  • ✕
